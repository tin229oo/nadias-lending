Web-Based Loan Management System

For Nadia's Lending Inc. — Justin Legaspi

Course: Web Design & Development Lab (T-CPET424)

Problem Statement

Manual loan processes lead to slow service, human error, and poor record keeping.

Objectives

  • Enable online loan applications
  • Automate loan computation and schedules
  • Provide admin approval & reporting

Demo Screens

  1. Landing / Register / Login
  2. Dashboard & Apply Loan
  3. Admin Approval & Reports

Conclusion

This solution reduces processing time, improves record accuracy, and increases transparency for both management and customers.
